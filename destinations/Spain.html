Barcelona, Spain

Junior year of college, I spent 5 magical months studying abroad in Barcelona. I loved the whimsy of being a tourist in the city I lived in. From meeting gypsies in the street to roaming the modern art galleries, every day was a new adventure. That's the magic of Barca and the Catalonian coast. Must dos include sangria at Ovella Negra, greasy sandwiches at la Champagneria, local jams at Jazzman, a Futbol Club Barcelona game at Camp Nou.
