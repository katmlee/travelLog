Panama

Bart and I were lucky enough to have Jeremy take us around Panama. After 2 years in the Peace Corps, Jer was basically a local and knew the hidden gems. Boquete is a spectacular spot for rainforest adventures. We scootered past double rainbows and lush plantations, zip-lined through chilly canopies, rode caballos on steep dirt paths and even stayed in a tree house. The coast is speckled with resorts that are ideal for some post-adventure r&r although the beaches aren’t the best in Central America. Panama City wasn’t too notable except for the fish market’s delicious ceviche.
