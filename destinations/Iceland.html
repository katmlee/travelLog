Iceland

Iceland in the winter is magical. I literally visited the the home of the "free folk" and Interstellar's Mann planet. The raw beatuy of Iceland's landscape is overwhelming; from plunging misty waterfalls to desolate iced-over lakes, every moment was picturesque. Although I wasn't lucky enough to see the Aurora Borealis on this trip, I was not dissapointed at all. If you're looking for the freshest seafood (or puffin), unlimited "blue-light" photo opportunities, or just a unique winter adventure, this is your destination.
