Morocco

Spring break 2010. From Barcelona, I took a cheap Ryan Air flight and spent Semana Santa at a surf camp in Morocco. This trip was the best combination of culture, new friends, partying, shopping, great food, and wanderlust. My favorite moment was groggily waking up in a caravan to see goats, REAL GOATS, jumping out of trees! Another memorable moment was getting scrubbed down by a topless women in a bath house with 10 other giggling girls. By far though, the best part of Morrocco was the kind people we befriended. Morrocco stole my eyes, my stomach, and my heart.
