Thailand

Thailand so vibrant in so many ways. Bangkok was our gateway to city, full of history, megamalls, tuktuks, massage parlors & STREET FOOD/Bars/Shops (basically anything you need can be found on the streets). From Bangkok, Ao Nang (airport Krabi) is a short flight away to paradise. It was an amazing retreat from the hustle and bustle of Bangkok to scooter around the lazy beach town and eating the best Pad Thai ever on the side of the road. The sunsets are incredible. Following Ao Nang we took a ferry to Koh Phi Phi which had incredible & cheap scuba diving although the party atmosphere on the island was overwhelming. Lastly, we went to Phuket. I'm not sure what all the rage is about though, Phuket felt super touristy unless you're staying at a 5-star resort. is like a mini Vegas plopped itself on strip of a beautiful beach. Anyways, to do it right I'd say you need at least 10 days but even then I didn't see enough.
