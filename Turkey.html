Turkey

Turkey is an incredible destination with so much variety. Sacred buildings in Istanbul have walls with literally layers of ancient history, the beaches are bordered with clear turquiose water, and Cappadocia's landscape is straight out of Star Wars. Turkey, for me, is the definition of adventure. As a female solo traveler I was worried about safety, but the people I met were incredibly friendly. I met a few locals at Saklient Gorge who took me on a crazy hike in waist-high rapids and drove me back to Fethiye on their scooter. The adventure never stopped from scuba diving, paragliding, horseback riding to hot air ballooning. Turkey took my breath away.
